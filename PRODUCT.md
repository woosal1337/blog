# PRODUCT.md

register: brand

## Product Purpose

Personal site of Ege Çelebi (chele.bi). Two jobs, in this order: a readable blog for longform writing, and a portfolio that presents projects (Clinora, Pulsar, Learnity, open source) on their own surface, separated from the writing. Secondary surfaces: music library, bookshelf.

## Users

1. Readers arriving from X or search, landing directly on a post. Often on a phone, often at night. They came to read, nothing else.
2. Founders, collaborators and recruiters skimming who Ege is and what he has built. Desktop, daylight, 30 seconds of attention.

## Brand and Tone

Apple, one to one, by explicit decision. The color system, type scale, pill buttons, frosted nav, flat tile elevation and motion restraint are taken directly from apple.com and Apple Newsroom (tokens extracted June 2026, recorded in DESIGN.md). Voice is direct, no filler, with proper sentence-case capitalization (Apple's own register). Dots and commas only in site copy, no dashes.

This identity commitment overrides generic anti-convergence heuristics. The system font stack and the Apple palette are the brand, not a reflex.

## Strategic Principles

- Readability beats everything. This is a blog first.
- One idea per fold. Generous space between sections.
- Animation is purposeful and rare. Nothing performs.
- Common components everywhere. Every new surface composes existing primitives before inventing new ones.
- Blog posts stay as MDX files exactly where they are. Redesign restyles them, never rewrites them.
- Both light and dark mode, system-following, with a persistent toggle.

## Anti-References

- The current site itself: "too crowded, too much information collapsing into each other, unnecessary information." The redesign exists to fix this.
- The retro 8-bit aesthetic (dashed borders, pixel fonts, kanji marquee, glitch and scramble effects). Retired on all new surfaces.
- Gamified self-presentation (skill bars, lvl badges, quests, guild log). Replaced with plain prose and quiet lists.
- Generic AI portfolio patterns: bento grids, gradient text, dark-with-purple-glow, oversized hover scales.

## Kept As Is

- The music page keeps its library data and dialogs, restyled to the Apple Music shape.
- Spotify now playing, contribution graph, books shelf: kept, restyled.
